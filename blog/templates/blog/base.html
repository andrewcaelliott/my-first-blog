About This Website

IsThatABigNumber.com:

  • Puts numbers into a context we can understand: billions and trillions, whether in science or economics, are hard to grasp. We try to reduce these to understandable comparisons.
  • Promotes numeracy through engaging articles about numbers and how they are used to describe, understand and control the world.
  • Lets you discover number facts, and share them.
  • Has an explicit educational objective, to develop a more advanced number sense in a gentle and entertaining way.
  • (For the techies) Has a web service API, allowing you to incorporate our content in your website
  • Is in a very early state of development!
  • Would love your support in any form: share the link, write to us, write about us, or donate to this project.
